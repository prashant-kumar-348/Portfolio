Modern Office Workspace

3D Modeling • 2024

Blender

Project Overview

This is a modern office environment created in Blender using a mix of custom models and free assets. I focused on clean lighting, natural window illumination, and realistic materials to build a warm and professional workspace. The scene highlights my skills in environment design, lighting setup, and render optimization.

My Contributions

    Environment Design

  • Built a modern office interior with a dual-desk layout.
  • Added furniture, plants, wall décor, and structural elements.
  • Combined custom models with free assets for a complete scene.

    Lighting & Atmosphere

  • Set up natural daylight through large windows.
  • Added supporting artificial lights for balanced illumination.
  • Adjusted shadows and reflections for a warm, realistic mood.
  • Integrated reflections and subtle bounce lighting to enhance the realism inside the room.

    Technical Work

  • Integrated assets and optimized scene composition.
  • Refined materials, shaders, and textures.
  • Optimized render settings for clean, noise-free output.

Tools & Technologies

  • Blender 3D (Modeling, Layout, Shading, Rendering)
  • Cycles Renderer (high-quality global illumination)
  • HDRI Lighting (for soft ambient bounce)
  • Free Asset Libraries
